# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors, recruiters, collaborators, and future clients who need to quickly understand Jax's background, project work, skills, notes, and contact paths.

## Product Purpose

This site is a personal portfolio and working archive. It presents project work, personal background, skill documents, notes, and contact information in a clear, modern, and maintainable web experience.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, and polished. The interface should feel current and confident without looking flashy, old-fashioned, or overly technical.

## Anti-references

Avoid heavy dark sci-fi styling, excessive glassmorphism, glowing borders, oversized capsule buttons, dense visual effects, and old portal-style layouts.

## Design Principles

- Make the first screen explain who this is and what the visitor can explore.
- Let content hierarchy lead; effects should support the reading path.
- Keep the visual system light, clean, and smooth with restrained dark accents.
- Preserve all existing editing, upload, document, and project workspace functions.
- Use responsive layouts that feel intentional on desktop and mobile.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect reduced-motion preferences, maintain readable text contrast, keep controls keyboard-accessible, and avoid layouts where long Chinese or URL text can overflow.
